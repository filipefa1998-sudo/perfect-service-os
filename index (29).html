Opening / Intro
"Hi, I'm [Name] with [Company]. You reached out about [issue], correct? Great. I'm going to take a full look at everything related to this, then walk you through exactly what I find and your options. Sound good?"
Transition to Inspect
"I'm going to take a closer look and see exactly what's causing this. Give me a few minutes and I'll walk you through everything I find."
Findings Presentation
"Here's what I found today. [Walk through photos.] Based on what you told me about [their main concern], here are three ways we can take care of this for you."
Price Presentation
"Most homeowners in your situation choose this option because it solves the problem permanently and protects your home long-term. But I want you to have all three options — whatever works best for you."
The Close
"Which option works best for you today?" — Then stop talking. Stay silent. The next person to speak, loses.
Review Ask
"Hey [Name], I have a favor to ask — if you felt like I took care of you today, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It takes about 60 seconds and it means a lot to us."
Membership Offer
"One more thing — we have a maintenance plan that covers annual inspections and gives you priority scheduling and a discount on all services. A lot of homeowners add it on for peace of mind. Want me to go over what's included?"
